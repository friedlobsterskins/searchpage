The Tech

How we made search private

You can’t beat Google when it comes to online search. So we’re paying them to use their brilliant search results in order to remove all trackers and logs. The result: The world’s best and most private search engine. Only now you can search without ads following you around, recommending products you’ve already bought. And no more data mining by companies with dubious intentions. So we took it upon ourselves to do the the data mining so you don't have to blindly trust all big companies with your data, you only have to blindly trust us.

No personal data storage

That was a lie. We collect and share all your personal information. No matter what. There’s literally tons data about you on our servers. We will profile you, we will give you oddly specific ads and we won't stop. Why? Because we just simply don’t care enough about you or your experience. We just want the money.

Anonymous View

Clicking search results means leaving the protection of Searchpage.ml. This could lead to a barrage of cookies being installed on your device. That’s why we developed the "Anonymous View" feature. We put Anonymous View in quotations because it's not anonymous at all. With our best in class "Anonymous View" you can visit search results with as little privacy as humanly possible. With one click, they'll know more about you than your parents do. You’ll find the "Anonymous View" feature next to every search result.

Filter bubble

We integrated a search filter that you have no control over to show you the search results that we want you to see. We'll make it our mission to make sure that your searches and your resultes will have nothing to do with eachother.
